Music App

My Music app a simple mp3 player.
Got the idea of making a mp3 player from Real Python Website.
I used Tkinter to build the app structure and Pygame to play the songs.
Also you are able to search songs from youtube I used "pyyoutube" to do youtube queries due doing scraping was not an option and download using "pytube" the audio this one needs to be converted to mp3 I used "moviepy" to convert it.
I used "mutagen.mp3" to get songs data like mp3 length.
